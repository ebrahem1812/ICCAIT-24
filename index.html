Artificial intelligence (AI) have tremendous impact on industry sector. ICCAIT'25 is a premier forum for AI and Industry community to present state of the art of the AI research and technology in Industry. The conference will bring together scientists, researchers and practitioners around the world to exchange their ideas and findings proposed to improve Industry and solve some of the Industry problems. Renowned speakers will be invited to present their findings and insights. Researchers are encouraged to submit original, unpublished contributions to be considered. ICCAIT'25 will be held in August 13-14, 2025 at Misr University for Science and Technology (MUST) in Giza, Egypt, which is located about 17 km from the great pyramids. The conference will be an excellent opportunity for exchanging ideas of future technologies and visiting great historical places in Egypt.

Location & Activities

ICCAIT'25 will be held at Misr University for Science and Technology (MUST) located in the 6th of October city, which is about 38 km west of Cairo, and 17 km from the great pyramids of Giza. A trip will be organized for interested to visit the great pyramids and other historical places.

Paper Submission

Authors are invited to submit original papers in Latex/Word format in the IEEE style. Templates can be downloaded from the IEEE website at this link: https://www.ieee.org/conferences/publishing/templates.html Manuscripts should be submitted electronically in the right format via the EasyChair conference account that can be accessed from the conference webpage.

Evaluation and Publication Process

Submitted manuscript will be evaluated blindly by two peer reviewers. Technical merits, novelty of the method, soundness of results, and clarity of presentation are important factors of the evaluation process. Accepted papers for authors who complete registration will be published in the conference proceedings that will be submitted for inclusion to IEEE Xplore.
